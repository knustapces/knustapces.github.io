About

This official website of Association of Petrochemical Enginnering Students(APCES) was created by the Tech and Digitalization Committee. The Tech and Digitalization Committee is a committee that was set up as part of the 2022/2023 adminstration.

The committee was set up to help promote the use of Information Technology in the petrochemical industry and other I.T applications. The first two years of the programme are the same as for the chemical engineering programme. It is intended to build the student background in chemistry, mathematics and general engineering. The core petrochemical engineering courses are introduced in the third year. The final year courses are devoted to the applications of the basic principles studied in the earlier years in pollution prevention and control, catalysis, corrosion, petroleum refining, and petrochemical process and plant design.

Flexible elective programs allowthe student to specialize in areas such as polymer technology, fertilizer technology, renewable energy processes, risk management, computer optimization methods, electrochemical technology, fuels and combustion technology, refining processes technology, natural gas liquefaction and handling, etc.

Graduates of the programme can pursue careers in the following: petroleum refinery industry (Tema Oil Refinery), Bulk Oil Storage and Transport (BOST), Ghana Standard Board, Environmental Protection Agency (EPA), CEPS. Petrochemical industries (e.g. fertilizer, bitumen, fabrics, and polymers), Asset integrity management services, Research and development agencies (e.g. CSIR, universities), Oil marketing companies (e.g. TOTAL, GOIL, SHELL)
