-------------APCES-------------

Association of petrochemical Engineering Student popularly known as APCES was established in 2019 to bring it members together.
The first two years of the programme are the same as for the chemical engineering programme. It is intended to build the student background in chemistry, mathematics and general engineering. The core petrochemical engineering courses are introduced in the third year. The final year courses are devoted to the applications of the basic principles studied in the earlier years in pollution prevention and control, catalysis, corrosion, petroleum refining, and petrochemical process and plant design.
